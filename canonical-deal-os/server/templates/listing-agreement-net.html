Net Listing Agreement

Commercial Real Estate Listing

IMPORTANT DISCLOSURE: This is a NET LISTING agreement. The Broker's compensation will be the amount by which the sale price exceeds the net amount specified below. Net listings may be prohibited or restricted in some jurisdictions. Both parties should consult with legal counsel before entering into this agreement. Seller acknowledges understanding that Broker has an incentive to sell at the highest possible price, and this creates a potential conflict of interest.

6. SELLER'S ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

Seller acknowledges and understands that:

  • Broker's compensation increases as the sale price increases above the Net Amount;
  • This arrangement creates an incentive for Broker to achieve the highest possible sale price;
  • Seller has the right to accept or reject any offer;
  • Seller has been advised to seek independent legal counsel;
  • Net listings may be prohibited or restricted in certain jurisdictions;
  • Seller has had adequate time to consider this agreement.
7. BROKER'S DUTIES

Broker agrees to:

  • Market the Property professionally and in good faith;
  • Disclose all offers to Seller promptly;
  • Act in Seller's best interest despite the net listing arrangement;
  • Provide Seller with a written calculation of commission before closing;
  • Comply with all applicable real estate laws and regulations.
8. DISCLOSURE OF NET LISTING

Broker shall disclose the existence of this net listing arrangement to all prospective buyers and their agents prior to presenting any offer.

10. TERMINATION

11. LEGAL COMPLIANCE

Both parties acknowledge that net listings may be subject to additional regulatory requirements or restrictions. If any provision of this Agreement violates applicable law, that provision shall be modified to comply with the law while preserving the parties' intent to the extent possible.
