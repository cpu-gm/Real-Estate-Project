The property shall be listed at an asking price of {{LISTING_PRICE}}, subject to negotiation.

3. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on {{START_DATE}} and expire on {{END_DATE}}, unless extended by written agreement.

4. COMMISSION

Upon the sale, exchange, or transfer of the Property during the term of this Agreement, Seller agrees to pay Broker a commission of {{COMMISSION}} of the gross sale price.

The commission shall be due and payable at closing. Broker's commission is earned if:

  • Broker procures a ready, willing, and able buyer;
  • Seller accepts an offer to purchase;
  • Any sale, exchange, or transfer occurs during the listing term, regardless of who procures the buyer.
5. BROKER'S DUTIES

Broker agrees to:

  • Use reasonable efforts to market and sell the Property;
  • Present all offers to Seller promptly;
  • Assist in negotiating terms acceptable to Seller;
  • Maintain confidentiality regarding Seller's financial information.
6. SELLER'S DUTIES

Seller agrees to:

  • Cooperate with Broker in showing and marketing the Property;
  • Refer all inquiries to Broker;
  • Provide accurate information about the Property;
  • Execute documents necessary for closing.
