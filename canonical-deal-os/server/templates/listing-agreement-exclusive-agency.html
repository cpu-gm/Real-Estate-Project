Exclusive Agency Listing Agreement

Commercial Real Estate Listing

NOTICE: This is an exclusive agency agreement. The Seller retains the right to sell the property directly without paying a commission. However, if any broker (including cooperating brokers) procures the buyer, commission is due.

THIS AGREEMENT is entered into as of {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}} between:

SELLER: {{SELLER_NAME}} ("Seller")
BROKER: {{BROKER_NAME}} ("Broker")
1. PROPERTY

Seller grants Broker the exclusive agency right to market and offer for sale:

Property Address: {{PROPERTY_ADDRESS}}
Property Type: {{PROPERTY_TYPE}}
Legal Description: {{LEGAL_DESCRIPTION}}
2. LISTING PRICE

The Property shall be offered for sale at an initial listing price of {{LISTING_PRICE}}, subject to adjustment by mutual written agreement.

3. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on {{START_DATE}} and expire on {{END_DATE}}, unless extended by written agreement or terminated earlier pursuant to this Agreement.

4. EXCLUSIVE AGENCY GRANT

Seller grants Broker the exclusive agency right to market and sell the Property during the Term. This means:

  • Broker Commission Due: If the Property is sold through the efforts of Broker, any cooperating broker, or any other real estate licensee during the Term;
  • No Commission Due: If Seller sells the Property directly to a buyer whom Seller procured without any assistance from Broker or any other real estate licensee.
5. COMMISSION

Seller agrees to pay Broker a commission as follows:

Commission: {{COMMISSION}}

Commission shall be earned and payable upon the earlier of: (a) closing of the sale; (b) Seller's default under a binding purchase agreement; or (c) Seller's refusal to close after accepting an offer meeting the listing terms.

Cooperating Broker Compensation: Broker is authorized to offer compensation to cooperating brokers of {{COOP_COMMISSION}} from the commission earned.

6. PROTECTION PERIOD

If, within {{PROTECTION_DAYS}} days after expiration or termination of this Agreement, Seller enters into a contract to sell the Property to any buyer who was introduced to the Property by Broker during the Term, Seller shall pay the commission set forth above.

7. SELLER'S RESERVED RIGHTS

Seller expressly reserves the right to sell the Property directly to a buyer procured solely by Seller's own efforts, without any involvement by Broker or any other real estate licensee, without obligation to pay commission.

8. BROKER'S DUTIES

Broker agrees to:

  • Market the Property professionally and diligently;
  • Provide regular updates on marketing activities and buyer interest;
  • Present all offers promptly to Seller;
  • Maintain confidentiality of Seller's financial and personal information;
  • Comply with all applicable real estate laws and regulations.
9. SELLER'S DUTIES

Seller agrees to:

  • Refer all buyer inquiries to Broker;
  • Provide accurate information about the Property;
  • Make the Property available for showings with reasonable notice;
  • Disclose all known material defects;
  • Notify Broker immediately if Seller is contacted directly by a potential buyer.
10. TERMINATION

This Agreement may be terminated:

  • By mutual written agreement;
  • By Seller upon {{TERMINATION_NOTICE_DAYS}} days written notice;
  • Immediately upon material breach by either party.
